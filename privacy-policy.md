Privacy Policy — LITLE CHAT
Last updated: 08 Jan 2026
LITLE CHAT is a private journaling application designed to provide a quiet and personal space for writing. Your privacy is a core principle of this app.
This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use LITLE CHAT.

1. Information We Collect
LITLE CHAT does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
The app stores:
* Journal entries you write
* A username you choose
* A security PIN (stored securely in hashed form)
This data is used only to provide the core functionality of the app.

2. How Your Data Is Used
Your data is used solely to:
* Allow you to access your journal
* Secure your entries behind a PIN
* Display your own writing back to you
Your journal content is never shared, never posted publicly, and never analyzed for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security
* Your journal data is stored securely.
* Access to your journal requires your PIN.
* If you forget your PIN, your data cannot be recovered for privacy reasons.
* Resetting the app will permanently delete your local journal data.
We do not provide data recovery or backdoor access.

4. AI Companion
LITLE CHAT may include a passive companion feature.
* The companion does not read or analyze your journal entries.
* It does not provide advice, diagnosis, or automated responses.
* It exists only as a quiet presence and can be dismissed at any time.

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.
We do not use your journal content for:
* Advertising
* Profiling
* Behavioral tracking

6. Analytics & Tracking
LITLE CHAT does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools to analyze journal content.
Basic technical logs may be used only to ensure app stability and security.

7. Children’s Privacy
LITLE CHAT is not directed at children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Control Over Data
You have full control over your data:
* You can delete individual journal entries at any time.
* You can reset the app, which permanently deletes all stored data.
There is no cloud-based recovery once data is deleted.

9. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated as the app evolves.
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the official project repository.
